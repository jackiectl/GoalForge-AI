🎮 Prediction Game

A just-for-fun game with virtual Coins — no real money, nothing at stake. Call the knockout ties using the model's own odds and see how your bracket does against reality and against the model itself.

🎯 How it works

  • You start with 1,000 Coins. For a tie, back the team you think goes through, and optionally call the exact 120' score for a much bigger reward.
  • Odds come straight from the GoalForge model (fair odds = 1 ÷ probability): longer odds pay more, because the model thinks it's less likely.
  • Right on who advances → paid at the outcome odds. Also nail the exact score → paid at the (much longer) score odds.
  • How many calls you get depends on when you join: Round of 32 → 4, Round of 16 → 3, Quarter-finals → 2, Semi-finals → 1. Spend them well.
  • Calls settle automatically against real results as they come in (the daily Live update). The 🤖 Model plays too, backing its own pick on every tie, as a benchmark.
