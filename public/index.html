ℹ️ How it works & what to trust

Win/draw/loss odds come from a 50/50 blend of a time-decayed Dixon-Coles model and a gradient-boosting outcome model — the lowest-error combination in a walk-forward bake-off over 11 major tournaments (2014-2024). Scorelines and player scoring/assist attribution stay Dixon-Coles, with player rates blending international records, club xG/xA, and position priors. The full-tournament page walks the single most likely path through the real 2026 format; the honors page adds Monte-Carlo probabilities from thousands of simulated tournaments. Hosts get a deliberately down-weighted home edge. Every layer is trained only on data from before the tournament kicked off (2026-06-11) — a genuine pre-tournament forecast, not betting advice.

Squads & caps/goals: Wikipedia (2026 World Cup squads, CC BY-SA). Team strength: martj42 international results. Club xG/xA: Understat (5 leagues). Player events: StatsBomb open data. Bracket rules: FIFA WC2026 Regulations (Annex C). Research demo — not betting advice.
